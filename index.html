Information of BMW M5 COMPETITION

The M5 Competition is a high-performance variant of BMW’s 5 Series, tuned by BMW’s M division. It's essentially the sportiest, most track-capable saloon in the 5 Series lineup—offering increased power, sharper chassis and suspension tweaks, more aggressive styling and upgraded features compared to the standard M5.

Key Specs & Performance

  • Engine:4.4-litre twin-turbocharged V8 petrol with BMW M TwinPower Turbo tech
  • Power:~ 625 PS (≈ 616-625 hp)
  • Torque:750 Nm
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph):~ 3.3 seconds
  • Top Speed:Limited to 305 km/h in many markets; this depends on options and governing speed limiters

Chassis, Handling and Drive Modes

  • Comes with M xDrive (BMW’s AWD performance system) which offers multiple modes. One mode can allow more rear-wheel bias or even full rear-wheel drive (“2WD mode”) for enthusiasts.
  • Suspension and chassis are retuned for sharper handling: stiffer engine mounts, revised dampers, stronger anti-roll bars etc.
  • Weight is higher owing to performance hardware, but the overall tuning aims to keep responsiveness high.

Style & Design

  • Exterior changes over standard M5 include more aggressive front/rear bumpers, gloss black accents on grille, ORVMs, quad exhausts, possibly a carbon fibre roof (depending on trim/market)
  • Enhanced lighting: laser headlights, smoked or LED tail lamps, new L-shaped DRLs etc.
  • Bigger wheels (often 20-inch) with sport tyres, and sporty interior touches: sport seats, M-steering wheel, M seats belts, trims etc

Features & Comfort

  • Infotainment: The facelifted Indian M5 Competition has a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster + 12.3-inch centre display, with BMW OS 7.0, wireless Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
  • Sound system: Harman Kardon surround sound in many variants; high-quality audio experience.
  • Other luxury and convenience: gesture control, ambient lighting, adjustable climate control, multiple drive modes etc.

India-Specific Pricing & Model Info

  • Ex-showroom price in India (facelifted M5 Competition) is about ₹ 1.62 crore
  • Earlier launch price was ~ ₹ 1.55-1.61 crore (ex-showroom) for prior versions.
  • here is a special limited edition called “50 Jahre M Edition” (50 Years M) priced higher (~₹ 1.80 crore), with exclusive styling, unique paint, special badging and additional luxury touches.
